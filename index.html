📐 Mathematics PYQs

Q1. Find the value of sin(45°) + cos(45°)

Options: A) √2 B) 1 C) 2 D) 0

Answer: A) √2

Explanation: sin(45°) = 1/√2, cos(45°) = 1/√2, Sum = 1/√2 + 1/√2 = 2/√2 = √2

Q2. Solve: 2x + 5 = 13

Options: A) x=3 B) x=4 C) x=5 D) x=6

Answer: B) x=4

Explanation: 2x = 13 - 5 = 8, x = 8/2 = 4

Q3. Find the area of a circle with radius 7 cm

Options: A) 154 B) 308 C) 77 D) 49

Answer: A) 154

Explanation: Area = πr² = 22/7 × 7² = 22/7 × 49 = 154 cm²

Q4. What is the derivative of x³?

Options: A) 3x² B) 3x C) x² D) 3

Answer: A) 3x²

Explanation: d/dx(x³) = 3x² (Power rule)

Q5. Find tan(60°)

Options: A) 1 B) √3 C) 1/√3 D) 0

Answer: B) √3

Explanation: tan(60°) = sin(60°)/cos(60°) = (√3/2)/(1/2) = √3

⚛️ Physics PYQs

Q1. A car travels 100 km in 2 hours. What is its average velocity?

Options: A) 50 km/h B) 100 km/h C) 200 km/h D) 25 km/h

Answer: A) 50 km/h

Explanation: Velocity = Distance/Time = 100 km / 2 hours = 50 km/h

Q2. Newton's Second Law states: F = m × a. What does 'a' represent?

Options: A) Distance B) Acceleration C) Velocity D) Force

Answer: B) Acceleration

Explanation: F = ma, where m is mass and a is acceleration

Q3. What is the SI unit of force?

Options: A) Kilogram B) Meter C) Newton D) Joule

Answer: C) Newton

Explanation: SI unit of force is Newton (N), where 1 N = 1 kg·m/s²

Q4. Calculate kinetic energy of an object: m = 2 kg, v = 3 m/s

Options: A) 6 J B) 9 J C) 12 J D) 18 J

Answer: B) 9 J

Explanation: KE = ½mv² = ½ × 2 × 3² = 1 × 9 = 9 Joules

Q5. What is the acceleration due to gravity on Earth?

Options: A) 8.8 m/s² B) 9.8 m/s² C) 10.8 m/s² D) 11.8 m/s²

Answer: B) 9.8 m/s²

Explanation: Standard acceleration due to gravity = 9.8 m/s² (approximately 10 m/s²)

🧪 Chemistry PYQs

Q1. What is the atomic number of Carbon?

Options: A) 4 B) 6 C) 8 D) 12

Answer: B) 6

Explanation: Carbon has 6 protons, hence atomic number = 6

Q2. What is the pH of a neutral solution?

Options: A) 0 B) 7 C) 14 D) 3.5

Answer: B) 7

Explanation: pH = 7 is neutral. pH < 7 is acidic, pH > 7 is basic

Q3. What is the molecular formula of water?

Options: A) H2O B) H2O2 C) CO2 D) O2

Answer: A) H2O

Explanation: Water consists of 2 hydrogen atoms and 1 oxygen atom

Q4. What is the valency of Oxygen?

Options: A) 1 B) 2 C) 3 D) 4

Answer: B) 2

Explanation: Oxygen has 6 valence electrons and needs 2 more electrons to complete its octet, so valency = 2

Q5. Balance the equation: C + O2 → CO2

Options: A) 2C + O2 → 2CO2 B) C + 2O2 → CO2 C) C + O2 → CO2 D) 2C + 2O2 → 2CO2

Answer: C) C + O2 → CO2

Explanation: 1 C atom + 2 O atoms → 1 CO2 molecule (already balanced)

📚 English PYQs

Q1. Choose the correctly spelled word:

Options: A) Acommodation B) Accommodation C) Acomodation D) Accomodation

Answer: B) Accommodation

Explanation: Correct spelling: Double 'c' and double 'm' = Accommodation

Q2. Find the antonym of "BRAVE":

Options: A) Courageous B) Cowardly C) Bold D) Strong

Answer: B) Cowardly

Explanation: Brave means fearless; Cowardly is the opposite

Q3. Find the synonym of "HAPPY":

Options: A) Sad B) Cheerful C) Angry D) Tired

Answer: B) Cheerful

Explanation: Cheerful means happy or in good spirits

Q4. Correct the sentence: "He go to school every day"

Options: A) He going to school every day B) He goes to school every day C) He gone to school every day D) He went to school every day

Answer: B) He goes to school every day

Explanation: With singular subject "He" and present tense, use "goes" (3rd person singular)

Q5. Choose the correct meaning of "ENIGMA":

Options: A) Clear B) Mystery C) Obvious D) Simple

Answer: B) Mystery

Explanation: Enigma means a mysterious or puzzling person or thing
